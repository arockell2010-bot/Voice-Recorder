Privacy

Voice Recorder is designed to keep recordings on your device.

Microphone Access

The app asks for microphone access only when you tap the record button. Android or your browser controls this permission.

Recordings

Recordings are saved in local browser storage on the device where they were created. They are not uploaded by this app.

Sharing

The app lets you download recordings. After download, you control where those files are stored or shared.

Data Removal

You can delete recordings inside the app. Browser data clearing or uninstalling the PWA may also remove saved recordings.
